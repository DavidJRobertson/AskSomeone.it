Rules and Information

  • Ask weird, interesting questions to strangers.
  • Your questions/answers are anonymous.
  • We reserve the right to publish questions/answers given.
  • We recommend you do not divulge personal information.
  • You must be at least thirteen years old to use this website.

What is AskSomeone.It?

It's a fun way to ask questions and get responses from other random people who are online.

Who is answering the questions?

Other people who are online! Each question you ask goes to a random person, so you might not get the same person twice!
